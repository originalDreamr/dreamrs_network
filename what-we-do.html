Dreamrs makes it easy for people all over the world to build organizations together, online.

  • Watch Demo
  • Join Us

DAOs made easy

Start an organization, give it structure, incentivize contributors, award reputation, and manage funds. No coding required.

Powerful on-chain governance

Smart, social decision making mechanisms help your organization get stuff done, avoiding voting where possible.

Open, flexible, and extensible

Dreamrs is open source and modular, enabling you to plug-in extensions to run your organization your way.

Blockchain Made Easy

User-friendly blockchain exchange for business owners

Dreamrs will automatically execute difficult tasks, such as equity distribution from a simple and user-friendly environment. Business owners and organization managers will have the flexibility in structuring their fundraising and operation strategies. For instance, YouTubers collaborate with Instagramers for shared profit.

Future Marketplace

Barter with business service providers

Dreamrs provides a marketplace for users to barter equity and future production for needed goods and services. Hence alleviate the burden of entrepreneurial start-up cost. For instance, a realtor can easily barter his or her future commission for accounting or legal services.

Regulatory Friendly

First regulatory friendly blockchain for organizations

Dreamrs will be proactive in building the bridge to communicate regulatory policies with communities needs.

Key Stakeholders

Network Extension Application Creator

The creator of the decentralized application built on the Dreamrs network while using any of the network ledger or overall infrastructure. The application creator is responsible for user acquisition and marketing in order to generate revenue in order to be able to pay network hosting fees

Validators

Consensus participants in the upcoming Dreamrs-Cosmos Hub who are responsible with securing the network and participating in the governance of the Dreamrs ecosystem.

Users

The end-user who wants to access a Dreamrs application or partner application.

Node Hosts

Community members intending to monetize on the provision of providing node hosting uptime.
